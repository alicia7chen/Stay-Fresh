General Storage Of Fruit


Tips for helping your produce ripen more quickly:
Store hard, unripe avocados in a brown paper bag on the counter.
Keep high-ethylene fruits near each other such as avocados, peaches, apples, cantaloupes, honeydew, bananas, tomatoes, pears, and plums.
Produce that sits together, ripens together
Tips for delaying ripening:
Fruits that are bruised or damaged ripen faster, so eat them first.
Wait to wash your fruits until youâ€™re ready to use them.
Fruits that kept in plastic will ripen faster, usually too fast. Store them loose.
What to store in the fridge:
Ripe fruits you want to keep fresh longer, Chopped fruit, Berries
What to store on the counter:
Bananas, stone fruits(such as plums, cherries, apricots), whole pineapples and melons, citrus
